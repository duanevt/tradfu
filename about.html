What is Tradfu?

Tradfu is your one stop solution platform. When you need something fixed or a problem solved then Tradfu will facilitate getting the right service to you - when you want! We take the hassle out of procuring and scheduling the services, acceptance and allocation of job requests and payment transactions.

It's great for service providers as it minimises the time and effort to secure jobs, does the scheduling and provides a framework for prompt and reliable payments.

It's great for customers who don't know where to look or haven't got the time to search for businesses that provide the quite often urgent jobs that need ordering. We provide a level of assurance and reliability to everyday people like you through our quality contractor rating system, eliminating dubious contractors.
